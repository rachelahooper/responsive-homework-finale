About me

Hello! My name is Rachel and I'm a nerd who's dealt with depression pretty much my whole life. This website is both a final responsive project for SheCodes and my attempt to put some therapeutic nerdery & mental health resources all in one place.

About this website

I needed a subject for my final responsive project and, given the current state of things, I needed to keep it real. My geeky obsessions and also geeking out on how to support my own mental health has kept me going through many tough times - now is no exception. I would love for this to help others as well. Again, not a mental health professional, just someone who's wanting to share what's worked for me.
